November 11, 2022 The Rest of God

I have always known that this life is not the end. That after we leave, we won't be strumming harps on the clouds everyday. So walking up and down these gravestones I know that I am reading it wrong. It is not "gone to be in the rest of God" as in relaxation, leisure, and ease. It is gone to be in the REST of God. Because there is more to Him. So much more.

Septermber 23, 2022 Nia's

Poems, Where do they come from? the inner mind? the subconscious? Or the universe? Even those writing may not know I don’t The words come Or they don’t They pull themselves from inside me Trying to get free Trying to release themselves They sometimes tug Or they yank It depends on what they have to say Or what I need to say There are days where I want them to come And others that they force themselves from me I want to be profound But mostly gibberish comes forth These poems are a journal In and of themselves They express how I feel Or what needs to be said In a way Only poems can show I don’t know what others would think When they saw my ramblings Would you think me crazy Or just a person Who can’t always express their feelings Expect through the words that come through poems

November 27, 2022 Stars

Late at night when men look to the stars They realize that these same sky's have faced the plights of their fathers come and gone. How many have stared into a moonless night and seen it's angel kissed face. How many have asked those heavens their deepest questions or dreamed upon a shooting star. It is powerful to know that the creator of that great shimmering expanse also created you. He has sent messages to those of ancient days and will answer the heartaches and prayers of the modern wanderers of the night.
